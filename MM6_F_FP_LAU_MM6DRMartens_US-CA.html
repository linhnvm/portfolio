MM6 Maison Margiela x Dr. Martens
 
MM6 Maison Margiela and Dr. Martens unveil their first collaboration for AW24, merging iconic footwear with avant-garde design.

This capsule reimagines classic Dr. Martens silhouettes - the original 1460 boot, the extra tall 1490 boot, the 1461 shoe, and the Penton loafer - blending Vintage Smooth and Virginia leather materials and textures for a bold, hybrid look. Each piece features signature MM6 numeric artwork and Dr. Martens iconic yellow welt stitch, offering a unique twist on heritage style.

Available now online.
